Frequently Asked Questions

What is 1:1 quality?

1:1 quality means our products are manufactured with the same materials, design, and attention to detail as the original luxury items, making them virtually indistinguishable.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes between 7-14 business days, depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for items that are unused and in their original packaging. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
